  • THIS IS THE LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT of me GLADYS EDITH YAXLEY of 269 Dereham Road Norwich Norfolk NR2 3TQ and I REVOKE all former Wills and Codicils and other testamentary dispositions made by me

     

    1. I DESIRE my body to be cremated

     

    2. I APPOINT my son Adrian COLIN YAXLEY of 40 Garfield Road Great Yarmouth Norfolk NR30 4JU to be the sole Executor of this my Will but if he shall die in my lifetime or shall refuse or be unable to act as Executor hereof then I APPOINT my sons's partner JILL BARRETT NORRIS of 40 Garfield Road aforesaid ("my trustee") to be the Executor and Trustee of this my Will

     

    3. I GIVE all my real and personal property whatsoever and wheresoever not specifically disposed of by this my Will or any Codicil hereto to my Trustees UPON TRUST to sell call in and convert into ready money all such parts of it as may not consist of money with absolute power to postpone sale without being liable for loss and after payment out of it of my just debts funeral testamentary and administration expenses to hold the net proceeds of such sale calling in and conversion and such part of my estate as shall be unconverted ("my Residuary Estate") UPON TRUST as to capital and income for my son the said ADRIAN COLIN YAXLEY absolutely but if my son ADRIAN COLIN YAXLEY predeceases me THEN UPON TRUST for my son's partner the said JILL BARRETT NORRIS absolutely

     

    4. IF no person shall attain a vested interest under the foregoing trusts of this my Will my Trustees shall hold my Residuary Estate UPON TRUST to divide into two equal shares as follows:-

     

    (a) One share UPON TRUST for CANCER RESEARCH UK of 61 Lincoln's Inn Fields London WC2A 3PX (Registered Charity Number 1089464) absolutely

     

    (b) One share UPON TRUST for the BRITISH HEART FOUNDATION of Greater London House Hampstead Road London NW1 7AW (Registered Charity Number 225971) absolutely

     

    5 (a) THE STANDARD PROVISIONS of The Society of Trust and Estate Practitioners (First Edition) shall apply with the deletion of:

    (i) paragraph 5

    (ii) the requirement for an independent Trustee from paragraphs 9(2) and 9(3) and

    (iii) "(other than a Professional Trustee)" from paragraph 12(1)

    (b) Section 11 of the Trusts of Land and Appointment of Trustees Act 1966 (Consultation with Beneficiaries) shall not apply

    (c) If any institution society or body of persons (incorporated or unincorporated) which is intended to be benefited by any gift made by me herein or in any Codicil hereto shall be found to never have existed or to have ceased to exist or to have been amalgamated with another institution society or body of persons or changed its name or constitution before my death the benefit intended under such gift shall be paid transferred or applied to such charitable institutions societies or bodies or persons established in the United Kingdom of Great Britain or Northern Ireland or for such charitable purposes in the said United Kingdom and if more than one in such shares as my Trustees in their absolute discretion think fit and I EXPRESS the wish without imposing any Trust or obligation that carrying out the foregoing trust my Trustees shall seek to benefit a charity or charities as close as possible in purpose to those of the institution to be benefited by my said original gift

     

    IN WITNESS whereof I have hereunto set my hand this 22nd day of March Two Thousand and Ten

     

    SIGNED by the above named

    GLADYS EDITH YAXLEY
